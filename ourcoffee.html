Our Coffee

We are proud of the coffee we serve. Our coffee is available in-store by the cup or by the pound. Our offerings include single-origin coffees, signature blends and an array of flavoured coffees.

Flavoured Coffee

French Vanilla, Butter Pecan, Irish Cream, and Vanilla Hazelnut.

Roasting

All of our coffee is roasted right here in Muskoka by Diesel House Coffee Roasters, our subsidiary company that was founded back in 2009! We roast in small batches every 2 weeks to ensure an extremely fresh, quality end product every time. Our coffee is made up of 100% Arabica beans and are roasted to perfection through a unique modern convection process.
