The Oliver’s Story

Welcome to Oliver's Coffee, where we're more than just a coffee shop – we're a part of the vibrant Muskoka landscape. Since 2003, we've proudly owned and operated multiple cafes, sprinkling Muskoka with the magic of our delicious coffee. You can find us in Bracebridge and our charming shops throughout Gravenhurst, Port Carling, Bala, and Huntsville.

Our recipe for success? It's no secret – we pour our hearts and passion into crafting our signature coffee and espresso blends roasted with love right here in the heart of Muskoka. Our mission is simple: from our local roaster to your cup, we serve you the freshest, best-tasting coffee.

But there's more to us than just coffee! Our team is dedicated to understanding the unique needs and preferences of our locals, cherishing our seasonal visitors, and offering a warm welcome to everyone walking through our doors. Community is at the heart of everything we do, and it's our honor to support local groups and events – because we're not just serving coffee, we're serving the very communities we're a part of.

Step into any of our shops and discover our delightful selection beyond just coffee. We offer mouthwatering sandwiches and an array of tasty treats, including donuts, muffins, scones, and more.

So, whether you're seeking a cozy leather chair to sip and surf, a quick zip through our speedy drive-thrus, or a delightful beverage and snack after a workout, we've got you covered. Wherever you are in Muskoka, get ready to savour a distinct and lovely coffee experience at Oliver's Coffee. See you soon!
